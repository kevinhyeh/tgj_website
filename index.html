How to Create Your Project Easily

Create a project with TGJ's free project builder in 3 easy steps.

1. Starting a project with us is easy. Just click on "Start Design" up top to begin.

2. We have several projects for you to pick from. If there's something you need but we don't have, just choose the "other" option.

3. Customize your project by choosing how complex you'd like it as well as the color scheme if you have one. Don't worry, it's optional.

4. Add some information about the project and yourself. We'd like to know as much information as we can to give you the best possible experience.

5. Once you submit then you're done. You can also add more projects if needed. Some of your information will still be there so you don't have to re-type them.

Choose Project

We provide a variety of projects for you to choose from. Is there something you need but we don't have? No problem. Go to the survey and click the "Other" option.

Fill Out Survey

We have spent countless hours to create an easy to use process where you can tell us exactly what you want for your project. Don't know what to say? No worries! You can tell us all about it either in person or over the phone when we contact you.

Send and Wait

That's it! You're done. We have received everything that you have filled out. Gives us some time to look over your information and we'll contact you either via email or phone within a one or two business days.

What forms of payment do we take?

Our main forms of payment are either through check, cash, Venmo, Paypal, or Square Cash. If there is another form of payment that you prefer, we can surely accommodate.

How many concepts do we provide?

We typically provide 2-3 concepts but if you don't love any of them, we will do another round on the house.

What's your cancellation policy?

Both parties reserve the right to cancel the project if deemed necessary. However any work that has been done must be accommodated. Don't worry though, we wont leave unless things get really bad.
